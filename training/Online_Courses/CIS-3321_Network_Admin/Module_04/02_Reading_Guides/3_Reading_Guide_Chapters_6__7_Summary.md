### 3. Reading Guide: Chapters 6 & 7 Summary
**High-Yield Concepts:**
1. **AAA Framework:** 
   * *Authentication:* Proving who you are (Passwords, Biometrics).
   * *Authorization:* What you are allowed to do once authenticated (Permissions).
   * *Accounting:* Tracking what you did (Logs).
2. **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):** Requires at least two different *factors*. Factors include: Something you know (Password), Something you have (Smartphone/Token), Something you are (Fingerprint).
3. **Port Security:** A Layer 2 switch feature that limits exactly which MAC addresses are allowed to plug into a specific physical port, preventing rogue devices.
