### 3. Reading Guide: Chapters 5 & 9 Summary
**High-Yield Concepts:**
1. **Cloud Service Models:** 
   * *IaaS (Infrastructure):* You manage the OS, provider manages hardware (e.g., AWS EC2).
   * *PaaS (Platform):* You manage the code, provider manages the OS (e.g., Heroku).
   * *SaaS (Software):* Provider manages everything, you just log in (e.g., Office 365).
2. **Antenna Types:** Omnidirectional (radiates in all directions, good for offices). Unidirectional/Yagi (focuses beam in one direction, good for building-to-building links).
3. **CSMA/CA:** Carrier Sense Multiple Access with Collision Avoidance. The protocol Wi-Fi uses to ensure devices don't talk over each other.
