### 4. Quiz 6 (Question Bank)
**Question 1:** What feature of WPA3 prevents offline dictionary attacks?
A) TKIP   B) SAE   C) AES   D) RADIUS
*   **Correct:** B) SAE (Simultaneous Authentication of Equals)
*   **Distractor Analysis:** TKIP is deprecated (WPA1). AES is the encryption cipher, but SAE is the handshake mechanism protecting the password. RADIUS is an authentication server.
