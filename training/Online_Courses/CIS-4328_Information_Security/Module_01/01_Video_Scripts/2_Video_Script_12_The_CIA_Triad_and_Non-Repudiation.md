### 2. Video Script 1.2: The CIA Triad and Non-Repudiation
**Estimated Duration:** 5 minutes
**Visual:** A triangle graphic. Point 1: Confidentiality. Point 2: Integrity. Point 3: Availability.
**[Alt-text: A triangle diagram labeled 'The CIA Triad'. The top point is Confidentiality, the bottom right is Integrity, the bottom left is Availability.]**
**Audio:** "The CIA Triad is the absolute foundation of cybersecurity. Confidentiality ensures data is only seen by authorized people—we achieve this with encryption. Integrity ensures data hasn't been altered—we achieve this with hashing. Availability ensures data is accessible when needed—we achieve this with backups and load balancers. There's also a fourth concept: Non-repudiation. This means a user cannot deny performing an action. If you digitally sign an email, you cannot later claim someone else sent it."
