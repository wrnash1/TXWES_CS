### 3. Reading Guide: Security+ SY0-701 Domain 4
**High-Yield Concepts:**
1. **Digital Forensics:** The process of gathering evidence. Order of Volatility is crucial: you must collect data from the most volatile sources first (CPU Cache -> RAM -> Swap File -> Hard Drive). If you turn the computer off, the RAM is wiped and evidence is destroyed.
2. **Chain of Custody:** A paper trail documenting exactly who handled forensic evidence, when, and where. If the chain is broken, the evidence is inadmissible in court.
3. **Disaster Recovery Sites:** 
   * *Hot Site:* Fully equipped, real-time data replication. Takes minutes to switch over. Expensive.
   * *Warm Site:* Has hardware, but data must be restored from backups. Takes hours/days.
   * *Cold Site:* Just an empty building with power and cooling. You bring the hardware. Takes weeks.
