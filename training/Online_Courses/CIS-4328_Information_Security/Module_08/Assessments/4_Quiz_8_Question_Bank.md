### 4. Quiz 8 (Question Bank)
**Question 1:** Which tool is designed not only to aggregate security logs and generate alerts, but also to execute automated playbooks to remediate threats without human intervention?
A) SIEM
B) SOAR
C) IDS
D) DLP
*   **Correct:** B) SOAR (Security Orchestration, Automation, and Response)
*   **Distractor Analysis:** A SIEM only alerts. A SOAR takes automated action based on those alerts. DLP prevents data exfiltration.
