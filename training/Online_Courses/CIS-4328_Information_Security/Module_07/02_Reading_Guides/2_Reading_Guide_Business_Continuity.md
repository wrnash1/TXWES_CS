### 2. Reading Guide: Business Continuity
**High-Yield Concepts:**
*   **Risk Responses:** 
    *   *Mitigation:* Buying a firewall.
    *   *Transfer:* Buying cybersecurity insurance.
    *   *Avoidance:* Disabling the vulnerable service entirely.
    *   *Acceptance:* Doing nothing because the fix costs more than the breach.
