### 4. Quiz 7 (Question Bank)
**Question 1:** A company determines that a specific server crashes an average of 4 times per year (ARO). Every time it crashes, it costs the company $2,000 in lost revenue (SLE). What is the Annualized Loss Expectancy (ALE)?
A) $2,000   B) $4,000   C) $8,000   D) $10,000
*   **Correct:** C) $8,000
*   **Distractor Analysis:** ALE = SLE x ARO. $2,000 x 4 = $8,000.
