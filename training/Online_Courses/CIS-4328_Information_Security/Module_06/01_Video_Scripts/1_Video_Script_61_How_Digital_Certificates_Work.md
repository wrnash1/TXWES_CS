### 1. Video Script 6.1: How Digital Certificates Work
**Audio:** "When you visit a secure banking website, how do you know the server actually belongs to the bank and not a hacker? Digital Certificates. A Certificate Authority (CA) acts like the DMV. The bank proves its identity to the CA, and the CA digitally signs the bank's public key, creating a Certificate. Your web browser trusts the CA, therefore it trusts the bank. This is Public Key Infrastructure."
