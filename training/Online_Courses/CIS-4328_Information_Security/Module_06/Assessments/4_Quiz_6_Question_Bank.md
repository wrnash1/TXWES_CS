### 4. Quiz 6 (Question Bank)
**Question 1:** A web server's private key was compromised by a hacker. The administrator immediately revokes the certificate. Which protocol will client web browsers use to verify in real-time that this specific certificate is no longer valid?
A) HTTPS
B) OCSP
C) CSR
D) IPsec
*   **Correct:** B) OCSP (Online Certificate Status Protocol)
*   **Distractor Analysis:** CSR is used to request a certificate. OCSP (and CRLs) are used to check the revocation status of a certificate.
