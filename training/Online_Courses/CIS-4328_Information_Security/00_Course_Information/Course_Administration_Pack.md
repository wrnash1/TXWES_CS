## 3. Updated Online Syllabus
**Course:** CIS 4328 - Information Systems Security
**Instructor:** Professor Nash
**Format:** 100% Online Asynchronous
**Target Certification:** CompTIA Security+ (SY0-701)

**Course Description:**
A comprehensive overview of cybersecurity principles and practices. Topics include threat actors, malware, social engineering, cryptography, network architecture, identity and access management, and security operations. 

**Required Materials:**
1. **Zero Textbook Cost (ZTC):** All required reading materials, official documentation, and study guides are provided completely free within the Blackboard course modules or via the `ZTC_OER_Reading_Materials.md` document. No textbook purchase is required.
2. Oracle VirtualBox with Kali Linux and Windows Server VMs for lab exercises.

**Course Objectives:**
By the end of this course, students will be able to:
1. Assess the security posture of an enterprise environment and recommend and implement appropriate security solutions.
2. Monitor and secure hybrid environments, including cloud, mobile, and IoT.
3. Operate with an awareness of applicable laws and policies, including principles of governance, risk, and compliance.
4. Identify, analyze, and respond to security events and incidents.

## 4. Grading Policy & Rubrics
**Grade Breakdown:**
*   **Weekly Security Labs:** 40%
*   **Weekly Quizzes:** 30%
*   **Final Certification Exam:** 30%

**Standard Lab Grading Rubric (100 Points):**
*   **Task Completion (50 pts):** The security tool (e.g., Nmap, GPG, ufw) was used correctly.
*   **Deliverable/Screenshot (30 pts):** Screenshot clearly demonstrates the output of the security assessment or configuration.
*   **Analysis (20 pts):** The student accurately interpreted the results (e.g., identifying the open ports or the compromised account).

## 5. Final Exam / Certification Instructions
**Location:** ComputerMinds Testing Center
**Format:** Proctored, In-Person
**Details:**
Your final exam is the **CompTIA Security+ (SY0-701)** exam. You must schedule your exam with ComputerMinds prior to Finals Week. 
*   **Passing the Certification:** Automatically receive a 100% (A) for the Final Exam grade.
*   **Failing the Certification:** Grade is prorated based on your numeric score relative to the passing threshold (750 on a scale of 100-900).
