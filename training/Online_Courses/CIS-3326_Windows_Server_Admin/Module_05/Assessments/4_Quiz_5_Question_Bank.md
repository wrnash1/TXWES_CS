### 4. Quiz 5 (Question Bank)
**Question 1:** Which DNS record type is specifically used by Active Directory to locate services, such as a Domain Controller?
A) A Record   B) CNAME Record   C) MX Record   D) SRV Record
*   **Correct:** D) SRV Record
*   **Distractor Analysis:** A records map names to IPv4. CNAMEs are aliases. MX records are for email servers. Service (SRV) records are required for AD clients to locate LDAP and Kerberos services.
