### 4. Quiz 6 (Question Bank)
**Question 1:** Which pricing model applies to BigQuery queries by default?
A) You pay per hour for the virtual machines provisioned to run the database.
B) You pay based on the total number of bytes read/processed by the query.
C) You pay a flat monthly fee regardless of usage.
D) You pay per database connection.
*   **Correct:** B) You pay based on the total number of bytes read/processed by the query.
*   **Distractor Analysis:** BigQuery is serverless (no VMs to pay for) and charges on-demand pricing based strictly on data scanned.
