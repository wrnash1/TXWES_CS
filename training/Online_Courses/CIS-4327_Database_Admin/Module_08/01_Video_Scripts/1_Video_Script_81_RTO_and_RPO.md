### 1. Video Script 8.1: RTO and RPO
**Audio:** "Disaster Recovery relies on two metrics: RTO and RPO. Recovery Time Objective (RTO) is how long the business can survive being offline. Recovery Point Objective (RPO) is how much data the business is willing to lose. If your RPO is zero, you must use synchronous replication. If your RPO is 24 hours, daily backups are sufficient."
