### 4. Quiz 8 (Question Bank)
**Question 1:** A financial institution dictates that in the event of a total data center failure, no more than 5 minutes of transactional data can be lost. Which disaster recovery metric does this requirement define?
A) Recovery Time Objective (RTO)
B) Recovery Point Objective (RPO)
C) Mean Time to Repair (MTTR)
D) Service Level Agreement (SLA)
*   **Correct:** B) Recovery Point Objective (RPO)
*   **Distractor Analysis:** RPO measures acceptable data loss (in time). RTO measures acceptable downtime.
