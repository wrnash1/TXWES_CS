### 3. Reading Guide: ACE Study Guide Domain 2
**High-Yield Concepts:**
1. **Preemptible VMs / Spot VMs:** Compute Engine VMs that run at a massive discount (up to 91% off) but can be terminated by Google at any time with only 30 seconds notice. Excellent for fault-tolerant batch processing, terrible for database servers.
2. **VPC Networks:** Virtual Private Clouds are global resources in GCP (unlike AWS where they are regional). Subnets are regional.
3. **Pricing Calculator:** Understand that you use the Google Cloud Pricing Calculator to estimate the monthly cost of a proposed architecture *before* you deploy it.
