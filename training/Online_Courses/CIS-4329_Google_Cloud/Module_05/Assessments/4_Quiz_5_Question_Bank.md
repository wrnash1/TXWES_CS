### 4. Quiz 5 (Question Bank)
**Question 1:** In Google Cloud Platform, what is the geographical scope of a Virtual Private Cloud (VPC) network and its subnets?
A) Both VPCs and Subnets are Global.
B) VPCs are Global, but Subnets are Regional.
C) VPCs are Regional, but Subnets are Zonal.
D) Both VPCs and Subnets are Zonal.
*   **Correct:** B) VPCs are Global, but Subnets are Regional.
*   **Distractor Analysis:** This is a core differentiator for GCP. A single VPC spans the globe, allowing subnets in different regions to communicate securely over Google's backbone.
