**Video Script 9.1: Deployments, Services, and Pods**
*Audio:* "In GKE, a Pod is the smallest unit—it holds your container. But you don't manage Pods directly; you manage Deployments. A Deployment ensures that if you ask for 3 Pods, exactly 3 Pods are always running. To expose those Pods to the internet, you create a Kubernetes Service of type LoadBalancer."
