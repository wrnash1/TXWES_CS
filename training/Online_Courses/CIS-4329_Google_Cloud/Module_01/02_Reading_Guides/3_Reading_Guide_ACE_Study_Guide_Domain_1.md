### 3. Reading Guide: ACE Study Guide Domain 1
**High-Yield Concepts:**
1. **Billing Accounts:** Projects consume resources, but Billing Accounts pay for them. A project must be linked to exactly one billing account to use paid services.
2. **Budgets and Alerts:** You can set budgets to send you an email when you hit 50%, 90%, or 100% of your expected spend. *Note: Budgets DO NOT automatically shut off resources—they only send alerts.*
3. **gcloud configuration:** Know the command to initialize the SDK locally: `gcloud init`. Know the command to check your current config: `gcloud config list`.
