### 3. Reading Guide: ACE Study Guide Domain 3
**High-Yield Concepts:**
1. **Cloud Marketplace:** A repository of pre-configured software stacks (like a WordPress blog or a LAMP stack) that can be deployed to Compute Engine with a single click.
2. **Custom Images vs. Snapshots:** A snapshot is a backup of a disk at a specific point in time. A Custom Image is a reusable template created from a disk to stamp out identical VMs in the future.
3. **GKE Autopilot vs. Standard:** In Standard GKE, you manage the underlying Compute Engine nodes. In Autopilot GKE, Google manages the nodes completely, and you only pay for the pods you deploy.
