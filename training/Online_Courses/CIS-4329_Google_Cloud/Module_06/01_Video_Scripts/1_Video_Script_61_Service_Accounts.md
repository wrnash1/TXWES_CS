### 1. Video Script 6.1: Service Accounts
**Audio:** "Identity and Access Management (IAM) controls human access. But what if a Virtual Machine needs to read a file from a Cloud Storage bucket? VMs don't have usernames and passwords. We use Service Accounts. A Service Account is an identity for an application or a VM. You assign the VM a Service Account, and you grant that account the 'Storage Object Viewer' role. This is the secure way to handle machine-to-machine authentication."
