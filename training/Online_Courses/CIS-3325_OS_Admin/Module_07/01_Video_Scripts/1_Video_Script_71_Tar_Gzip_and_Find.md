### 1. Video Script 7.1: Tar, Gzip, and Find
**Audio:** "When you download software in Linux, it often comes as a `.tar.gz` file, affectionately called a tarball. `tar` groups multiple files into one. `gzip` compresses it to save space. To find files hidden deeply in the system, we use the `find` command, which is vastly more powerful than the Windows search bar."
