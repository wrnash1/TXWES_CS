### 1. Video Script 8.1: GRUB and the Boot Process
**Audio:** "When you press the power button, the BIOS hands off to the Bootloader. In Linux, this is almost always GRUB (GRand Unified Bootloader). GRUB loads the kernel into memory, which then mounts the root filesystem and starts the `systemd` process. If your server won't boot, GRUB is where you intervene."
